First Light Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: First Light stores no personal data for its core features. Your location is used only on your device to calculate legal shooting times. The optional Cull Diary requires an account and stores your cull records securely in Supabase. We do not track you, serve ads, or sell data.

What we collect

Core app (Species, Legal Times, Calendar, Field Guide, Deer School) — no personal data collected:

  • No account required
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No cookies placed on your device
  • No advertising identifiers collected

Cull Diary (optional) — requires an account:

  • Email address and password for authentication
  • Cull records you enter (species, date, location, weights, photos, notes)
  • All diary data is stored securely in Supabase and only accessible by you
  • You can delete any entry or your entire account at any time

Your location

If you grant location permission, your GPS coordinates are used on your device only to calculate sunrise, sunset, and legal shooting times. This calculation happens entirely within your browser.

Your coordinates are never transmitted to any First Light server. The only external call made with location data is to OpenStreetMap Nominatim (a public geocoding service) to look up your nearest place name — this is a standard, anonymous request with no persistent identifier.

You can also set your location manually using the search or preset locations, in which case no GPS data is used at all.

Third-party services

First Light uses the following external services to function:

Open-Meteo
Weather forecasts. Your approximate coordinates are sent to fetch weather data. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API that does not store personal data. open-meteo.com
OpenStreetMap Nominatim
Place name lookup for reverse geocoding your location. Requests are anonymous. nominatim.org
Google Fonts
The app loads DM Sans and Playfair Display typefaces from Google Fonts. Google may log the request IP address. policies.google.com/privacy
Supabase (Cull Diary only)
If you choose to use the Cull Diary feature, your diary entries and any uploaded photos are stored securely in Supabase — a GDPR-compliant cloud database. An account (email and password) is required. Your data is stored under your user ID and is only accessible to you. Supabase processes data in accordance with GDPR. supabase.com/privacy

Data storage

First Light uses your browser's localStorage to remember your last-used location (coordinates and place name), active tab, and whether you have seen the legal disclaimer. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

If you use the Cull Diary feature, your cull records and photos are stored in Supabase under your account. Supabase also stores your authentication token in localStorage to keep you signed in between sessions — this token never leaves your device except when communicating with Supabase. This data is associated with your email address and is protected by row-level security — only you can access your own entries. You can delete any entry or your entire account at any time.

No data is stored on any First Light server at any point.

UK GDPR

First Light is operated from the United Kingdom and is subject to UK data protection law. The core app collects no personal data. If you use the Cull Diary, your account email and diary entries are processed on the lawful basis of consent — you choose to create an account and enter data. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. This policy is provided transparently in the spirit of UK GDPR compliance.

Children

First Light is intended for adults involved in or learning about legal deer management in the UK. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from minors.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that. As we collect no personal data, such changes are unlikely to affect you.
